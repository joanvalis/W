From the deep south into the far north

Laughs at this latitude require a Southern attitude to life. Raised in an island for almost 20 years, Joan's jokes range from phallocentric dark humor to asinine exploration of the Finnish daily life.
Completely devoid of proselytizing political undertones, Joan wants to make you laugh and forget about your problems, not give you a patronizing sermon on everything that's wrong with the world.
The weather inside Joan's soon-to-be-bald head is an eternal Spring, where poking fun at everything with playful banter comes off naturally and helps us get closer to each other. Vittuilu on välittämistä.
